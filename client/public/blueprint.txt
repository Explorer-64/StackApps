## IDENTITY
name: StackApps
tagline: Get your app verified. Backlink, StackApps Verified badge, and site readiness scan — free.
description: StackApps is a vetted public registry and verification layer for AI-ready,
  browser-ready indie apps (The Stackhouse). Any builder can get their app verified for free.
  When approved as live, three things happen automatically: (1) a permanent server-rendered
  proof page is created at stackapps.app/apps/[slug] with a dofollow backlink to the live
  app URL — present in raw HTML, visible to every crawler on first load; (2) an embeddable
  StackApps badge is available as an SVG at stackapps.app/api/badge/[appId].svg and displays
  StackApps Verified when verification checks pass; (3) a Site Readiness technical audit
  checks 10 signals (site reachability, llms.txt, robots.txt, sitemap.xml, FAQ presence,
  Android PWA, iOS PWA, service worker, viewport meta, Blueprint Protocol) and stores
  results publicly on the proof page. Apps that pass all 10 checks are directed to
  StackLaunch (stacklaunch.app) for promotion. Apps with failing checks are directed to
  StackLaunch AIEO/GEO for a prioritized fix list. StackApps is the free value layer.
  StackLaunch is the paid promotion and audit engine it feeds.
category: developer-tools / ai-ready-registry
url: https://stackapps.app
contact: support@stackapps.app
public-routes: /, /dashboard, /apps/*, /for-builders, /faq, /privacy, /terms
auth-required-routes: /hub, /settings, /workstation, /admin

## LISTING PAGES — TWO URLS, TWO PURPOSES
note: Every live-approved app has two URLs that serve different audiences.
  - stackapps.app/apps/[slug] — Server-rendered (SSR). Returns complete HTML on
    first load including the dofollow backlink, JSON-LD schema, and Open Graph tags.
    This is the canonical SEO URL and what crawlers index.
  - stackapps.app/app/[id] — Client-rendered SPA. The interactive listing with
    readiness scan results, badge embed tool, reviews, and rating form. This is
    what agents should navigate to for UI automation.
